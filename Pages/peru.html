Macchu Picchu

Let's Know about Macchu Picchu

  • Wonder's Name:Macchu Picchu
  • Location:Cusco Region of Peru

Features of Macchu Picchu

Macchu Picchu-Inca Stonework

  • Machu Picchu is famous for its intricate dry-stone construction. The precisely cut stones fit together without mortar, showcasing advanced Inca engineering.

Macchu Picchu-Temple of the Sun

  • The Temple of the Sun is a semi-circular structure believed to have been used for astronomical and religious purposes. Its windows are aligned with solar events, reflecting its importance in Inca cosmology.

Macchu Picchu-Intihuatana Stone

  • Its perfect symmetry, including the iconic central dome and four minarets, is a marvel of Mughal architecture
